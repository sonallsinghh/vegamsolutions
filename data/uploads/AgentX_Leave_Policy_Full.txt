AgentX – Leave Policy & Guidelines

Purpose
The purpose of this Leave Policy is to define the rules, procedures, and guidelines governing employee leave at AgentX. The company recognizes that adequate rest and personal time are essential for maintaining productivity, morale, and overall employee well-being. This policy ensures consistency, transparency, and fairness in leave administration. It supports employees in balancing professional responsibilities with personal needs while maintaining operational efficiency. This document serves as an internal guideline applicable to all eligible employees of AgentX.

Scope
This policy applies to all full-time employees of AgentX across departments and locations unless stated otherwise. Certain provisions may differ for interns, consultants, contractual staff, or part-time employees based on their employment terms. Any exception must be approved by the Human Resources department in writing. The scope of this policy may be revised based on business or statutory requirements.

Definitions
AgentX refers to the company and its affiliated entities. Employee refers to any individual employed by AgentX and on its payroll. HR refers to the Human Resources department. Leave Year refers to the calendar year from January 1 to December 31. Manager refers to the immediate reporting authority.

General Leave Rules
All leave must be applied through the official HR system. Prior approval from the reporting manager is mandatory except in emergencies. Leave approval depends on business requirements. Employees are encouraged to plan leave in advance. Misuse of leave may result in disciplinary action.

Leave Eligibility
Leave eligibility begins after completion of probation unless otherwise specified. Leave entitlements reset annually. Employees on notice period may have restricted leave access. HR reserves the right to determine eligibility in exceptional cases.

Types of Leave
AgentX provides multiple leave categories to address diverse employee needs. Each leave type has defined eligibility and limits. Leave types are not interchangeable unless specified. HR maintains all leave balances.

Earned Leave
Earned Leave is accrued monthly at 1.5 days per month. Maximum accumulation is 30 days. Excess balance beyond the cap may lapse. Earned Leave requires prior approval. Earned Leave may be carried forward within limits.

Usage of Earned Leave
Earned Leave may be used for vacations or personal reasons. Minimum usage is half a day. Earned Leave may be clubbed with other leave types. Encashment, if applicable, follows company policy. Balances are visible in the HR portal.

Casual Leave
Casual Leave is intended for short-term personal needs. Employees are entitled to 12 days annually. Casual Leave cannot be carried forward or encashed. Casual Leave lapses at year-end. Early intimation is encouraged.

Sick Leave
Sick Leave is provided for illness or medical conditions. Employees receive 10 days annually. Medical proof may be required for extended Sick Leave. Sick Leave cannot be carried forward or encashed. HR may verify medical claims.

Emergency Leave
Emergency Leave applies to unforeseen situations. Employees must inform their manager as soon as possible. Emergency Leave may be adjusted against available leave balances. Post-approval may be required. Abuse of emergency leave is prohibited.

Maternity Leave
Maternity Leave is provided in accordance with applicable laws. Eligible employees may avail up to 26 weeks. Medical documentation is required. Salary during maternity leave follows statutory norms. HR provides support throughout the period.

Paternity Leave
Paternity Leave is granted to eligible male employees. Up to 10 working days may be availed. Leave must be taken within three months of childbirth. Prior approval is required. Leave cannot be carried forward.

Marriage Leave
Marriage Leave is granted for the employee’s own marriage. Employees may avail up to 5 working days. Marriage Leave is allowed once during employment. Proof may be required. Leave must be availed within one month of marriage.

Bereavement Leave
Bereavement Leave supports employees during loss of immediate family members. Up to 5 days may be granted. Compassion and flexibility will be exercised. HR approval is required. Additional support may be provided.

Compensatory Off
Compensatory Off is granted for working on holidays or weekly offs. Prior approval is mandatory. Comp-Off must be availed within 60 days. Comp-Off cannot be encashed. Unused Comp-Off lapses.

Leave Without Pay
Leave Without Pay applies when leave balances are exhausted. Prior approval is required. Salary deductions apply. Benefits may be impacted. Excessive LWP may affect performance reviews.

Probation Period Leave
Leave during probation is limited. Casual Leave may be granted on a pro-rata basis. Earned Leave is not applicable. Sick Leave may be allowed in genuine cases. HR approval is required.

Notice Period Leave
Leave during notice period is discouraged. Earned Leave requires management approval. Casual Leave is generally not permitted. LWP may be considered. Final approval rests with HR.

Leave Application Process
All leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal. Correct leave type must be selected. Supporting documents must be uploaded if required. Managers must act promptly. HR audits records periodically.

Approval Authority
Immediate managers approve leave requests. HR resolves disputes. Senior management approval may be required for extended leave. Approval is not automatic. Business continuity is prioritized.

Leave Cancellation
Employees may cancel approved leave through the HR system. Managers must be informed. Frequent cancellations are discouraged. HR may review patterns. Misuse may lead to action.

Public Holidays
AgentX observes designated public holidays. Holiday lists are published annually. Holidays may vary by location. No leave application is required. Working on holidays may qualify for Comp-Off.

Record Maintenance
HR maintains leave records. Employees can view balances online. Discrepancies must be reported immediately. Records are confidential. Tampering is prohibited.

Policy Violations
Misrepresentation of leave is misconduct. Disciplinary action may be taken. Repeated violations may affect employment. HR investigations are final. Zero tolerance applies.

Policy Amendments
AgentX may amend this policy at any time. Changes will be communicated officially. Amendments override previous versions. Employee acknowledgment may be required. Continued employment implies acceptance.

Interpretation
HR is the final authority for interpretation. Clarifications must be sought from HR. Verbal assurances do not override policy. Legal compliance is mandatory. Ambiguities will be resolved by HR.

Confidentiality
Leave data is confidential. Access is restricted. Data misuse is punishable. Privacy laws are followed. Employee confidentiality is respected.

Acknowledgment
Employees must acknowledge this policy. Digital acknowledgment may be required. Non-acknowledgment does not exempt compliance. Queries should be directed to HR. Understanding is employee responsibility.

Conclusion
AgentX values employee well-being and work-life balance. Responsible leave usage is encouraged. This policy supports a healthy workplace. Employees should plan leave responsibly. HR remains available for support.
